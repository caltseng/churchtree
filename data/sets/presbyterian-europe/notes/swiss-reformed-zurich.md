# Swiss Reformed (Zurich stream)

## Summary
- The Zurich Reformation was initiated in the early 1520s by Huldrych Zwingli and became an early center of Reformed Protestantism in Switzerland.

## Key Dates
- start_year: early 1520s
- end_year: unknown
- Zwingli's life: 1484-1531

## Relationships
- Parent/precursor bodies: Late medieval Roman Catholicism in Zurich and the Old Swiss Confederacy.
- Split or merge relationships: Break from Roman Catholic authority in Zurich; influence spread to other Swiss cantons.

## Distinctives
- Memorialist view of the Lord's Supper and iconoclasm in worship.
- Close integration of civic authorities and church reform in Zurich.

## Size / Scale
- Estimated size (if known): unknown

## Events
- Early 1520s: Zwingli's reform program adopted by Zurich magistrates.
- 1531: Zwingli's death in the Canton of Zurich.
